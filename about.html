Our Story

🕉️ Our Journey

From Devotion to Service

Yatrakaar was born from a deep-rooted passion for spiritual travel and a simple realization — most yatra organizers offer rigid packages with little room for personalization. Devotees, especially families and elders, face immense stress coordinating travel, accommodations, and darshans instead of focusing on their spiritual intent.

Our founders, with combined 20+ years of experience in hospitality, yatra coordination, and on-ground management, came together to create something different — a service where every pilgrimage is as unique as the devotee undertaking it.

Based in Hyderabad, we specialize in organizing yatras across India & Nepal — from the snow-clad Char Dham in the Himalayas to the ancient temples of South India, from Ayodhya-Kashi-Prayagraj to Nepal's Pashupatinath & Muktinath, and thrilling Himalayan treks. Every journey is fully customized to your preferences, comfort, and schedule.

📅 History & Milestones

How We Grew

Our decade-long path of helping devotees travel with ease

2014

The Spark of Service

Sai Vardhan Reddy B begins organizing on-ground logistics and hospitality services. He starts offering personalized freelance consulting to major yatra organizers across the country, building a massive network of local vendors.

2017

Expanding Coordination

Venu Gopal Raj S joins the fold, specializing in A-Z coordination and on-ground problem-solving. Together, they handle complex high-altitude arrangements for Himalayan treks and temple tours in difficult terrains.

2022

Administrative Excellence

Ravikanth Matpathi coordinates end-to-end admin tasks, handling registrations, flight/train bookings, and quick doubt clarifications. A structured operation takes shape in Hyderabad to serve pilgrims transparently.

2025

YatraKaar Launch

The three founders officially establish YatraKaar, introducing a brand dedicated purely to premium, fully-customized yatras and spiritual tours, ensuring every single journey is planned with complete honesty and devotion.
