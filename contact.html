❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common queries to help you prepare for your journey

Can I fully customize the travel dates and hotel levels?

Yes! All our yatras are 100% customized. You can select your starting date, hotel level (from basic clean ashrams and dharamshalas to luxury premium resorts), transportation details, and sightseeing spots. We design the tour exclusively for you.

Do you provide accompanied on-ground coordinators?

Absolutely. For larger family groups or senior citizens circuits, our co-founders (Sai Vardhan or Venu Gopal) or certified local spiritual guides personally travel with you to handle hotel check-ins, VIP ticket slots, local languages, and coordinate meal logistics.

How do I book helicopter tickets for Kedarnath?

Helicopter bookings for Kedarnath are regulated by the state portal. Our administration lead Ravikanth coordinates the booking process on your behalf as soon as the state windows open, handling all documentation and verification steps smoothly.

Is biometric registration required for Char Dham Yatra?

Yes, biometric registration is mandatory for all pilgrims traveling to Uttarakhand for Char Dham. As part of our comprehensive services, we take care of the entire registration process for your group, providing you with ready-to-use registration cards before you travel.
